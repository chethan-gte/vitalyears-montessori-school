Our Programs

Age-appropriate Montessori programs designed to nurture every dimension of your child's development.

🍼
Ages 2 – 3 yrs
Playgroup

A warm first step into Montessori life — building motor skills, sensory awareness and emotional confidence through guided play.

🌱
Ages 3 – 4 yrs
Nursery (Montessori Level 1)

Children grow in language and independence through hands-on practical-life activities and meaningful social interaction.

📚
Ages 4 – 5 yrs
Junior KG (Montessori Level 2)

Carefully designed Montessori materials introduce early math and language concepts, nurturing curiosity and concentration.

🎨
Ages 5 – 6 yrs
Senior KG (Montessori Level 3)

A school-readiness program that strengthens problem-solving, communication and confidence as children prepare for the next big step.
